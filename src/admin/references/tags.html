Tag Reference Notes
Arcade "arcade"
Blitz "blitz"
Capture Points "cp" Hills that need to be captured to win - don't award points.
Capture the Flag "ctf"
Capture the Wool "ctw"
Destroy the Core "dtc"
Destroy the Monument "dtm"
Free-for-all "ffa"
Frenzy "frenzy" Avicus alternative to Blitz.
Ghost Squadron "gs"
King of the Hill "koth" Hills that award points that contribute to a total score.
Micro "micro" Also known as '5x5'.
RAGE "rage" Any map that uses the RAGE concept - blitz rage maps must also have "blitz" as a tag.
Ranked Edition "ranked"
Race for Wool "rfw"
Skywars "skywars"
Team Deathmatch "tdm"
TNT "tnt" Maps that are heavily focused around TNT and cannoning.
Tournament Edition "te"
Seasonal Edition "season": "" Requires a string, such as Christmas or Halloween, which is displayed as the tag.
Other gamemode or sub-gamemode "other": "" Requires a string, such as Juggernaut from Overcast's arcade, which is displayed as the tag.
