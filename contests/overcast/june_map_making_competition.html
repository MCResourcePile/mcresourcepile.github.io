    Details and Guidelines
    Only CTF and KoTF maps were allowed, allowing for 10v10 gameplay.
    Maps being created were to be designed for tournament environments.
    Upon the approval of an Event Coordinator, select contest maps would be included in Overcast Network's 10th tournament.
    XML documents were to be included with the submission.
    Prizes
    First PlaceMap residence, winner trophy, and a donor rank
    Second PlaceMap residence, and a donor rank
    Third PlaceMap residence, and a donor rank
