    Contest Information
    Dates
    Submissions openJanuary 29, 2022
    Submissions closeJanuary 29, 2022
    Public testingJanuary 29, 2022
    Results announcedJanuary 29, 2022
    Scoring (Total scores are a percentage out of 100%)
    Public rating out of 5 (weighted 20%)
    Gameplay out of 10 (weighted 40%)
    Aesthetics out of 10 (weighted 25%)
    Innovation out of 10 (weighted 15%)
    Additional Bonus of 3% for XML with minimal errors
    Details and Guidelines
    Maps could only be destroy (dtm/dtc), and can be for any team size.
    Maps were to be dry, and could not contain an excess of TNT.
    XML documents were to be included with the submission.
    Authors were allowed to be author on up to 3 maps, but only their top scored map was counted.
    Maps previously submitted to Stratus Network could not be entered into this contest.
    Prizes
    First PlaceStrato, winner tag on discord
    Second Place1 month of Stratus-, runner up tag on discord
    Third Place1 stack of tokens
