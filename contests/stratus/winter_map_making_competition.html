    Contest Information
    Dates
    Submissions openSeptember 06, 2022
    Submissions closeSeptember 06, 2022
    Public testingSeptember 06, 2022
    Results announcedSeptember 06, 2022
    Scoring (Total scores are the sum of each criteria)
    Gameplay out of 40
    Aesthetics out of 30
    Innovation out of 20
    Additional Bonus of 3% for XML with minimal errors and proper conventions
    Additional Bonus of 3% for map gamemodes in high demand
    Additional Bonus of 2% for map gamemodes in medium demand
    Additional Penalty of -5 points for author not following the guidelines
    Details and Guidelines
    Information Thread: https://stratus.network/forums/topics/5c4138059e299e00010050f0
    Maps could be any gamemode, but must be between 4v4 to 20v20.
    Maps must be in a team format, no FFA.
    XML documents were to be included with the submission. XML must be at least protocol 1.4.0.
    Authors were allowed to be author on up to 3 maps, but only their top scored map was counted.
    Maps previously submitted to Stratus Network could not be entered into this contest.
    Prizes
    First PlaceStrato, 5x Set Next tokens, winner trophy, winner tag on discord, guaranteed spot in Rotatation Update #20
    Second Place3 weeks of Nimbus, 5x Set Next tokens, runner up tag on discord
    Third Place3 weeks of Nimbus, 3x Set Next tokens
