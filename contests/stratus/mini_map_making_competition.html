    Contest Information
    Dates
    Submissions open April 4, 2017
    Submissions close May 15, 2017
    Public testing May 20, 2017
    Results announced May 21, 2017
    Scoring (Total scores are a percentage out of 100%)
    Public rating out of 5 (weighted 20%)
    Gameplay out of 10 (weighted 40%)
    Aesthetics out of 10 (weighted 30%)
    Innovation out of 10 (weighted 10%)
    Additional Bonus of 3% for XML with minimal errors
    Details and Guidelines
    Maps could be any gamemode, but must be no larger than 16v16.
    XML documents were to be included with the submission.
    Authors were allowed to be author on up to 3 maps, but only their top scored map was counted.
    Maps previously submitted to Stratus Network could not be entered into this contest.
    Prizes
    First Place Strato, 3 months of Stratus+, winner tag on discord
    Second Place 3 months of Stratus+, runner up tag on discord
    Third Place 3 months of Stratus-
